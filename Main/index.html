In today's world, pollution and resource scarcity are critical issues. Just one litre of used oil can contaminate a staggering one million litres of fresh water, endangering the supply for 50 people for a year. However, used oil isn't just waste-it's a valuable resource. Through the concept of Circular Economy, we can re-refine used oil to create lubricants, conserving precious petroleum reserves and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. This not only promotes a safer and healthier future but also helps combat climate change by minimizing the extraction and burning of fossil fuels in manufacturing processes, thereby reducing carbon footprints in our ecosystem.
